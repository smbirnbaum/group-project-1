Get to know me

My introduction to music production happened in a living room, watching a friend experiment with a turntable while building his own remix of a popular dance track. What started as curiosity quickly turned into a deep obsession with sound — with how a transition can shift emotion, how a hook can control a room, how timing can either break momentum or elevate it.

I saved up for my own deck and began studying everything I could: beatmatching, phrasing, cross-fading, harmonic mixing, seamless outros, building tension, releasing energy. I wasn't just learning to play tracks — I was learning how to tell a story through them.

That passion led me to the Musicians Institute in Hollywood, where I graduated in 2009 as a trained audio engineer. There, I refined both the creative and technical sides of music — sound design, mixing, mastering, signal flow, and the science behind what makes music hit the way it does.

Since graduating, I've worked professionally with music in multiple capacities, always blending technical precision with instinctive crowd awareness. My background in audio engineering means every set is intentional: clean transitions, balanced sound, dynamic progression, and zero dead air. Every mix has structure. Every build has purpose. Whether it's high-energy club sets, curated lounge atmospheres, weddings, corporate events, or private celebrations, I focus on one thing: reading the energy and elevating it.

If you're looking for a DJ who understands both the art and the science of sound, let's create something unforgettable.
